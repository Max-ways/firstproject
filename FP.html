Tribute To Chandwick Boseman

He played THE BLACK PANTHER role in Avengers

Born and raised in South Carolina,

Boseman attended Howard University where he studied fine arts.

He began his career acting, directing, playwriting, and teaching in New York City.

His first leading role in a Hollywood film was in 42.... His final film, Ma Rainey's Black Bottom,

will be released posthumously on Netflix.

Boseman died on August 28, 2020, after privately dealing with colon cancer for four years.
